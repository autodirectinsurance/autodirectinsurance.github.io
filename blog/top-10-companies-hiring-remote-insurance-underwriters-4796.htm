Top 10 Companies Hiring Remote Insurance Underwriters

Home / Blog / Blog Details

The insurance industry is undergoing a massive transformation, driven by technological advancements, shifting workforce expectations, and global economic uncertainties. One of the most notable trends is the rise of remote work, particularly for specialized roles like insurance underwriting. Companies are increasingly embracing remote underwriting teams to tap into global talent, reduce overhead costs, and improve efficiency.

If you're an insurance underwriter looking for remote opportunities, you're in luck. Below, we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 companies hiring remote insurance underwriters in today’s competitive market.

Why Remote Underwriting is Booming

The Shift to Digital Workflows

With AI-powered underwriting tools and cloud-based platforms, insurers no longer require underwriters to be physically present in an office. Automated risk assessment models allow remote underwriters to evaluate policies efficiently while maintaining accuracy.

Cost Efficiency for Employers

Hiring remote underwriters eliminates the need for large office spaces, reducing operational costs. Companies can also access a broader talent pool without geographical restrictions.

Work-Life Balance for Employees

Remote underwriting offers professionals flexibility, eliminating long commutes and allowing them to work in environments where they’re most productive.

Top 10 Companies Hiring Remote Insurance Underwriters

1. Lemonade

Lemonade, a disruptor in the insurtech space, leverages AI to streamline underwriting. They frequently hire remote underwriters to support their digital-first approach.

Why Work Here?
- Cutting-edge AI tools
- Fast-paced, innovative culture
- Strong focus on customer experience

2. Progressive Insurance

Progressive is a leader in auto insurance and has embraced remote underwriting to expand its workforce.

Why Work Here?
- Competitive salaries and benefits
- Strong training programs
- Opportunities for career growth

3. Allstate

Allstate has been a pioneer in remote work, offering flexible underwriting roles across various insurance lines.

Why Work Here?
- Well-established remote work policies
- Comprehensive benefits package
- Emphasis on employee wellness

4. AIG (American International Group)

AIG hires remote underwriters for commercial and specialty insurance, catering to global clients.

Why Work Here?
- International exposure
- High-profile clients
- Strong professional development programs

5. Chubb

Chubb is known for its elite underwriting standards and frequently recruits remote professionals.

Why Work Here?
- Prestigious reputation in the industry
- Competitive compensation
- Focus on underwriting excellence

6. Travelers Insurance

Travelers offers remote underwriting roles in property, casualty, and specialty insurance.

Why Work Here?
- Strong company culture
- Robust risk management tools
- Employee recognition programs

7. State Farm

State Farm has expanded its remote workforce, including underwriters who assess personal and commercial risks.

Why Work Here?
- Strong brand recognition
- Employee-centric policies
- Opportunities for internal mobility

8. Liberty Mutual

Liberty Mutual provides remote underwriting positions with a focus on innovation and customer service.

Why Work Here?
- Flexible scheduling
- Emphasis on diversity and inclusion
- Advanced underwriting technology

9. Nationwide

Nationwide hires remote underwriters for auto, home, and business insurance.

Why Work Here?
- Strong work-life balance initiatives
- Competitive bonuses
- Collaborative team environment

10. Hippo Insurance

Hippo, a modern home insurance provider, relies on remote underwriters to support its tech-driven model.

Why Work Here?
- Fast-growing startup environment
- Data-driven underwriting approach
- Employee stock options

How to Land a Remote Underwriting Job

Polish Your Resume

Highlight your underwriting experience, technical skills (e.g., risk assessment software), and remote work adaptability.

Network in Digital Spaces

Join LinkedIn groups, attend virtual insurance conferences, and engage with industry professionals online.

Prepare for Virtual Interviews

Since remote roles rely heavily on digital communication, ensure you’re comfortable with video interviews and virtual collaboration tools.

Stay Updated on Industry Trends

Remote underwriters must be proficient in AI, machine learning, and regulatory changes affecting the insurance landscape.

The demand for remote insurance underwriters is higher than ever, and these top companies are leading the charge. Whether you're an experienced underwriter or looking to transition into the field, now is the perfect time to explore remote opportunities.
